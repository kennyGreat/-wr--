Terms & Conditions

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to DataBulge, a service of Vortexedge Limited ("we", "us", "our"). By creating an account or using our platform, you agree to these Terms & Conditions in full.

2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

DataBulge is an installment-based mobile data purchasing platform. We allow users to subscribe to data plans and pay in daily installments over 7 or 28 days. A service surcharge applies to all installment plans.

3. ELIGIBILITY

To use DataBulge you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age, or have consent of a parent or guardian
  • Be a resident or legal user of mobile telecommunications services in Nigeria
  • Provide accurate, complete, and current information during registration
  • Have a valid Nigerian mobile phone number for data delivery
  • Have access to a valid payment method

4. ACCOUNT SECURITY

When you register, you agree to:

  • Create only one (1) account per person
  • Provide a valid email address and secure password
  • Keep your login credentials confidential
  • Notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access

5. HOW IT WORKS

5.1 Selecting a Plan

Browse plans by data size (500MB–50GB), network (MTN, Airtel, Glo, T2), validity period, and payment duration.

5.2 Installment Structure

  • 7-Day Plan: 5% surcharge. Total divided into daily installments over 7 days.
  • 28-Day Plan: 10% surcharge. Total divided into daily installments over 28 days.

5.3 Wallet & Funding

Each user maintains a personal wallet. Fund via bank transfer to your dedicated virtual account. Funds are held individually and applied automatically toward your active subscription.

5.4 Data Delivery

Once your wallet reaches the full plan amount, data is automatically purchased and delivered to your registered phone number. You will receive an in-app notification on delivery.

6. REFUNDS & CANCELLATIONS

Surcharges are non-refundable once a subscription is created. Wallet balance refunds may be requested within 7 days of funding if no data delivery has been made. Contact support at support@databulge.com.

7. PRIVACY

We collect your name, email, phone number, wallet transaction history, and KYC information where applicable. Data is not shared with third parties except as required by law or to provide the service.
